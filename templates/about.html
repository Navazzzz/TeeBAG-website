What's all this about?


As you might've gathered, this is a site for original Text Based Adventure Games. But also, this site is Navazzzz's Final Project for HarvardX's CS50: Introduction to Computer Science course.



But, why TeeBAG? But, why TeeBAG? Your time to shine.

Submit a custom-made game


Fill in the three fields below to submit your own Text Based Adventure Game to our website, and we'll get back to you ASAP. We'd also like you to know that,

  • If you select 'Other' for the genre below, you will be prompted later to specify the genre(if you'd ike to) when we get back to you via email
  • If you have experience with HTML and PYTHON, we will send you a layout of how the game is to be structured via email
  • If you do not have any HTML experience, you can send us the story of your game and we will implement it for you
  • For any other inquiries, you can contact us at creatorteebag@gmail.com
We'll never share your email with anyone else.
